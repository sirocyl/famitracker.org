Welcome to famitracker.org, the not-really-new home of the FamiTracker community.

What is FamiTracker?
FamiTracker is a tool for Windows, for making music on the NES and Famicom system.

For now, the site ain't much to look at, so give these links a look:

  • NSFPlay, the de facto NSF format player for PC.
  • Famicompo archives from previous years' events. temporarily unavailable - request access on Discord, please!

You can join the ft.o Discord server here.

We will plan on adding more to this site in the future, including but not limited to:

  • a forum for posting and discussing chiptune stuff.
  • a wiki for gathering together information and documentation.
  • a blog for a curated magazine-format view of the community, keeping up with news, advice and tips on workflow, and cool new updates to tools.
  • a place to post and share your music, where you can upload an FTM/0cc module, NSF, or audio for sharing your music in either format.

Stay tuned for more updates!
